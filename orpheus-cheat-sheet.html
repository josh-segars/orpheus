Your Signal, at a Glance

Five priorities and a weekly rhythm — prepared for Jane Doe
The Five Priorities
1
Grow Your Follower Base
Connect with 10–15 targeted professionals per week. Reply to every comment within 24 hours. Target: 1,000 followers in 90 days.
2
Establish a Publishing Rhythm
Post Monday, Wednesday, Friday — non-negotiable. Build a 2–3 week content bank so weekly publishing isn't a weekly decision.
3
Request Recommendations Strategically
Identify 3–5 colleagues from your most recent and prestigious roles. Send specific, personal requests. Target: 2 new recommendations in 30 days.
4
Publish Your First LinkedIn Article
Write one 800–1,200 word piece at the intersection of your expertise and your audience's biggest current challenge. Share it as a post with a short excerpt.
5
Sharpen Your Content Voice
Before every post answer: what is my situation, what do I think, why does that matter to my audience? Cut updates and shares that have no personal angle.
Your Weekly Rhythm
Every Day
  • Reply to any new comments within 24 hours.
  • Spend 10 minutes engaging with target-audience posts.
Every Week
  • Publish Mon / Wed / Fri from your content bank.
  • Send 10–15 targeted connection requests.
  • Review your top post of the week for patterns.
Every Month
  • Send one personal recommendation request to a former colleague, client, or supervisor.
  • Draft the next article — even if it doesn't publish this month, keep the pipeline alive.
  • Check follower growth, posts-per-week, and engagement rate against your baseline.
